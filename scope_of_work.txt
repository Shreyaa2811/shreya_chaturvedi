Scope of work:
The project involves the planning, design, development, testing, and deployment of a fully functional online gaming website. The website will include user registration and authentication, profile management, a searchable game catalog, integration of multiple HTML5 casual games, score tracking, achievement management, favorites functionality, and leaderboard systems. The project also includes the development of an administrative dashboard for managing users, games, and platform content. Additional tasks include implementing responsive design, security features, performance optimization, and basic analytics to ensure a reliable and user-friendly gaming experience. The focus is on creating a centralized gaming portal rather than developing complex standalone games.
